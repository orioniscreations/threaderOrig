After her Mother's disappearance, Kida Solvieg hears word that her Father, the King of Galar, has been assassinated... and by none other than her brother, the Crown Prince Youta. However, Kida knew how close her brother and their father was, and could not bring herself to believe it. To get to the bottom of it, she sends out several messengers to find out the truth, and return to her... but, when not a single one of them return, her fears drive her to take the journey to Galar herself. In order to make the journey, Kida hires a Rover who has experiene traversing the dangerous Wheld between the two Kingdoms...

However... it appears someone does not want them to make it to their destination, and will do whatever it takes to stop them. Can Kida, and the Rover, Ares, make it through the Wheld, find her brother, clear his name, and save her Brother's Kingdom?

Accused of his father's assassination, Youta fled the Capitol, leaving it in the hands of a userper. With no one to depend on, other than his loyal retainer, Thanion, he must find a way to prove his innocence, and find a way to expose the userper as the villain he is.

After a request for assistance is sent from Turffield, the Castle sent only one man - an Otherworlder named Tatsu - to answer the request. Upon arriving, the Lady of Turffield explained to Tatsu that a 'monster' has made its home in the mines, thus making it impossible for the Miners to work, and it was cutting off Turffield's majoy source of income.

Using his equipment from his own world, Tatsu must face off against this 'monster' in order to save the town.

While attempting to travel between Dimensions, Tatsu and Noel accidentally end up in the wrong Universe! Now, seperated and stuck in a dying branch universe, they have to team up with some familiar-yet-different faces in order to find each other, and get back to their own timeline!

Five years ago, Balia's boyfriend, Miguel, was murdered in a robbery-gone-wrong, and during his funeral, his little brother ran away, and was never seen again. Near the five year anniversary of his death, Balia decided to visit his grave, only to find someone oddly familiar standing in front of it... who seemed to be haunted by a mysterious Ghost. The Ghost seemed to linger after the boy left, and seemed to be interested in Balia? But what does it want with her?

Mika's life should have ended five years ago - on the day of his brother's funeral. Instead, he ended up making a pact with Death to become her host, and somehow gained immortality. Unable to die or grow ill, Mika decided to spend his time in the forest, away from the humans that had brough nothing but ruin to his life... that is, until an old childhood friend managed to track him down with information about his brother's murderer.

Craving revenge, Mika left the shelter of the forest, and embarked on a quest to find his brother's murderer, and destroy them.
